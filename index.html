Introduction

Jeph C. Njikonye
SAN & Co.

Jeph C. Njikonye SAN & Co. is a core legal practice Law Firm with its head office in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja and correspondent offices in Lagos, Port Harcourt and Umuahia. It was established in 2003 by the Principal Partner, Mr. Jephthah C. Njikonye, SAN.

We are a multi-specialty legal service provider with a track record of about (20) years in active legal practice. We are a highly motivated law firm and we provide hands-on, practical, research and result-oriented services, advice, counsel, solution and assistance over a diverse and multi-facetted legal problems and issues. Our legal team is made up of result-oriented and dedicated legal minds in the industry with very broad experience/expertise in various aspects of litigation and non-litigation resolution processes.
